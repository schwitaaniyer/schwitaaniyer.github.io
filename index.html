About Me

I’m a Systems Engineer Executive at Hughes, building next-generation, software-defined and autonomous networking solutions. With a background in Engineering Physics and Computer Science, my core interest lies in designing intelligent systems that perceive, decide, and act autonomously in complex environments.

My research interests center on Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, and Autonomous Systems, particularly decision-making under uncertainty, multi-agent systems, and AI-driven control and autonomy. During my research experience at IISER Bhopal, I explored autonomous and computational approaches to complex physical systems, which strengthened my drive to bridge theory with real-world robotic and AI applications.

I actively upskill in LLMs, Generative AI, AI agents, and autonomous system architectures, with the goal of building scalable, adaptive, and intelligent solutions. I’m driven by the belief that impactful innovation emerges at the intersection of research, engineering, and collaboration—and I aspire to contribute to technologies that reshape industries and empower a more intelligent, connected world.
